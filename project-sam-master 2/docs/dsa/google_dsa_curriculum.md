## **Daily Routine:**

### **Morning (60-90 minutes):**
1. **New Problems:** 2-3 problems from today's list
2. **Pattern Focus:** Understand the core pattern, not just the solution

### **Evening (30-45 minutes):**
1. **Review Problems:** 1-2 problems from previous days
2. **Quick Log:** 3-line summary of progress

### **Weekly (Day 5, 10, 15, 20):**
1. **Mock Test:** 3 problems, 45 minutes
2. **Pattern Review:** Identify weak areas
3. **Next Week Planning:** Adjust difficulty if needed

---

## **Success Metrics:**
- **Daily:** 2-3 new problems + 1-2 review problems
- **Weekly:** 80%+ success rate on review problems
- **Monthly:** Can solve 90%+ of problems within 15 minutes

**Remember:** Quality > Quantity. Understanding patterns > memorizing solutions.
