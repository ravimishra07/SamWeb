# Behavioral Interview Preparation

**Purpose:** Comprehensive behavioral interview preparation for Google interviews using STAR method.

---

## **Week 1: Leadership & Teamwork**

### **Day 1: Leadership Stories**
**STAR Framework:**
- **Situation:** Describe the context
- **Task:** What was your responsibility
- **Action:** What did you do specifically
- **Result:** What was the outcome

**Key Stories to Prepare:**
1. **Leading a team through a difficult project**
2. **Mentoring a junior developer**
3. **Taking initiative on a project**
4. **Handling team conflict**

**Practice Questions:**
- "Tell me about a time you led a team"
- "Describe a situation where you had to motivate others"
- "How do you handle team disagreements?"

### **Day 4: Problem Solving & Innovation**

---

## **STAR Method Template:**

**Situation:**
- Set the context (when, where, who)
- Keep it concise but specific

**Task:**
- What was your responsibility?
- What was the goal or objective?

**Action:**
- What did you do specifically?
- Focus on your actions, not team actions
- Use "I" statements

**Result:**
- What was the outcome?
- Quantify when possible
- What did you learn?

---

## **Success Metrics:**
- **Daily:** Complete 2-3 polished STAR stories
- **Weekly:** Successfully complete mock behavioral interview
- **Monthly:** Can confidently answer any behavioral question

**Remember:** Authenticity > Perfection. Be genuine in your responses.
